Upcoming Game Titles I'm Excited For

Here are some upcoming game titles that I'm really excited about!

Black Ops 6

I usally dont get too excited for new cod releases, but treyarch is my favourite studio and with the announcment of a return to normal round based zombies i am pretty excited for this game but i never get my hopes up to high for cod titles.

Gta 6

I still play gta 5 to this day, i think its one of the greatest games of all time, and GTA 6 has had far more development time and has been revealed to have a much larger map, so i am excited for all the new things that will be available.

Dragon Ball Sparking Zero

This game is a revival of my favourite dragon ball series, the tenkaichi series. i used to play those games all day on the ps2 and for some reason they never remastered or re released those games so i wasnt able to play them anymore, having the series reboot is a dream come true for me.
