About This Website

This is a website I have created for Assignment #1 of CPAN 134, where I will be discussing one of my favourite hobbies, which is gaming.

How I Started

My gaming journey began when I was 5 years old playing various story mode games on my PS2. I played Ratchet and Clank, Sly Cooper, and Jak and Daxter. Those were my favourite games. A few years later, when I turned 7, my brother bought us a PS3, and from there I started playing his copy of COD 4 Modern Warfare, which is what got me into first-person shooter franchises. From there, I played every single Call of Duty up until WWII. After which, I took a 2-3 year break from gaming. About 3-4 months ago, I picked up an Xbox and started playing the older Call of Dutys again because Activision was acquired by Microsoft, and they revived the servers. That revived my old love for gaming, and I’ve been playing more consistently these days on my PS5. I just recently built a new gaming PC that I will be using as well.

Why I Enjoy Gaming

The main reason I enjoy gaming is mostly the competitive fun that comes with it. After a long day, I like to hop on with my friends and play some sort of objective multiplayer game, usually either Black Ops Zombies or Fortnite, and sometimes GTA 5. I feel like after a long day, it’s a nice escape and destressor. However, I only really have about 1 hour a day available to even play, but when I was a kid, I played for hours a day. It’s kind of hard to describe why exactly I enjoy it so much, but it’s just a fun experience.
